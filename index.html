Research Interest: Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), XR (VR/MR/AR), Human Perception, Illusion, Haptics, Dream, Game

I am a master student from National Taiwan University, and I am looking for a Ph.D. position. To explore more about HCI, I have worked with Lung-Pan Cheng and Li-wei Chan and published 3 papers (CHI'20, IEEEVR’21, and UIST’21). With these experiences, I’m more determined to devote myself to HCI.

To make VR more immersive, I plan to combine human perception and VR, which includes : (1) utilizing dreams as a medium for VR , and (2) hacking human perception for VR players.

In addition to research, I also love to make games during spare time.
