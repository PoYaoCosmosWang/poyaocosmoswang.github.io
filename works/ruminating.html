Ruminating

  • Game Link: https://joanxia.itch.io/ruminating
  • Award: 2019 Drunk Game Jam Best Art Prize
  • Authors: Po-Yao (Cosmos) Wang, Meng-Ju Chung, Xuan (Joan) Xia, Hsin-Yu Huang, Guan-Wei Chen, Hao-Jen Chen
  • Time: Sep. 2019
  • Category: Game

In the Ruminating, you can choose three foods to eat. Drink some beer and start your Ruminating!

After ruminating, you will get new foods.

Try different food combinations and collect new foods!

Po-Yao (Cosmos) Wang, 2021
