Intelligent Eye-screen Protector

  • Award: 1st prize of smart life group in 2016 HackNTU
  • Github: asd00012334/HackNTU_2016 (github.com)
  • Authors: Po-Yao (Cosmos) Wang, Yu-Hsuan Huang, An-Fong Hwu, Yong-Xuan Wang
  • Time: Aug. 2016
  • Category: Hackthon

Intelligent Eye-screen Protector is a Hackathon project that helps computer users to quit their inappropriate behavior of using computers. We mainly focus on two rules: (1) the distance between the eyes and the screen should be appropriate (distance = diagonal (inch)*2.54 * 1.5) , and (2) users should take a rest after using computers for 40 minutes. Hence, we created our intelligent eye-screen protector with Arduino to detect the distance.

We also built a reward system for the users to make sure users are willing to adjust their posture. They can level up their Tamagotchi if they keep themselves in a good posture.

Po-Yao (Cosmos) Wang, 2021
