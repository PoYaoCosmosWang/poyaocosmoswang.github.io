Eternal Melody

  • Game Release: Github
  • Authors: Po-Yao (Cosmos) Wang, Guan-wei Chen, Hao-Jen Chen, Yu-Hsien Lo
  • Time: Dec. 2016 - Jun. 2017
  • Category: Game

Eternal Melody is a rhythm battle game. Players have to tap the attack button according to the music. Enjoy the music and BEAT the enemies!

Cast special skills by simple gestures at the right time!
